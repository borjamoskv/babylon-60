# CEP-000: BABYLON-60 Architectural Constitution

**Status**: FROZEN
**Type**: Standards Track (Constitution)
**Created**: 2026-06-29

## Abstract
This document defines the irrevocable constitutional principles of the BABYLON-60-Persist microkernel architecture. It serves as the foundation for all subsequent BABYLON-60 Engineering Proposals (CEPs) and reference implementations. No future CEP, regardless of consensus or technical merit, may violate the rules established herein.

## 1. The 7 Constitutional Articles

**Article 1: Inviolability of MI-001**
MI-001 (No artifact generated by the system itself constitutes independent evidence of the claims it contains) is absolute and inviolable. No CEP shall modify, attenuate, or introduce an exception to MI-001. Any CEP that implies a violation of MI-001 is automatically invalidated without the need for voting or discussion.

**Article 2: Structural Guarantee Limitation**
The BABYLON-60 Microkernel shall ONLY guarantee structural integrity, cryptographic provenance, and DAG consistency. It shall NEVER guarantee semantic correctness or truth.

**Article 3: Principle of Explicit Non-Guarantees**
Every technical guarantee asserted in any future specification MUST be accompanied by an explicit non-guarantee. No clause may claim a capability without explicitly enumerating its boundaries and what it does not protect against.

**Article 4: Reversibility**
Every architectural design decision outside of this document (CEP-000) is considered reversible. The principles defined in CEP-000 are the only irreversible axioms of the BABYLON-60 ecosystem.

**Article 5: Permanent Agnosticism**
The standard shall remain permanently agnostic regarding any specific trust algebra, domain model, or concrete algorithmic implementation. BABYLON-60 defines the parameterizable space, not the operations within it.

**Article 6: Conformance Testability Constraint**
No feature, abstraction, or mechanism shall be added to the standard if it cannot be deterministically tested by an executable conformance test suite.

**Article 7: Narrative Independence**
Narrative coherence, conversational consensus, or logical fluency do not constitute valid evidence for any modification of the standard or validation of an implementation. Truth is bound exclusively to cryptographic integrity and empirical testing.

## 2. Meta-Normative Framework
BABYLON-60 does not solve the problem of computational epistemology. It converts the problem of computational epistemology into an engineering problem. The architecture is an instrument of inspection to prevent epistemic decisions from remaining implicit.
