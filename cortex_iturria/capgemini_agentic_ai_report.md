# [KNOWLEDGE] Capgemini Report 2025: Rise of Agentic AI

## 1. Core Primitives (O(1) Definitions)
- **Agentic AI vs GenAI Assistants**: AI agents proactively manage and execute end-to-end processes independently without explicit prompts for every step, marking a shift from *helpers* to *team members*.
- **Economic Value**: Projected to generate up to $450 billion across surveyed countries by 2028, driven largely by operational efficiency, sales growth, and accelerated R&D.
- **Adoption Paradox**: While 14% of organizations claim scaled implementation (and 23% pilot), expectations for autonomy remain low. By 2028, only 25% of processes are expected to reach Level 3+ autonomy.
- **The Trust Deficit**: Trust in fully autonomous agents dropped from 43% in 2024 to 22% in 2025. This indicates a reality check. Less than 20% report mature data-readiness or AI infrastructure, limiting advanced autonomy.
- **Build vs Buy Strategy**: 62% prefer partnering with enterprise solution providers (e.g., Salesforce, SAP, ServiceNow) for pre-integrated agents rather than relying completely on custom in-house agent development.
